FACULTY ESTABLISHMENT

The Faculty of Computers & Information, Fayoum University, was established under the presidential decree No 267 of 2006.
In its session dated 9/3/2006, the Supreme Council of Universities (SCU) consented to begin the study in the faculty in the academic year 2006/2007.

FACULTY LOGO

The Logo of the Faculty of Computers & Information consists of two parts: the first part represents the famous Wheels of Fayoum; the second represents Information Technology which is composed of two digits (1, 0). These two parts symbolizes the leap in IT form analogue system to digital system.

VISION

The faculty of Computer Sciences and Information looks forward to achieve excellence and distinction in the areas of education and scientific research to reach a highly distinctive status locally and internationally, as well as developing the community in terms of information, together with adhering to ethics of the career.

Mission

The faculty of Computer sciences seeks to prepare highly qualified staff in the field of computers and Information technology, who are qualified and fully equipped with the theoretical and applied basics in the specialty area and always willing to learn to compete in the job market in the context of moral values and contributing to the development of the scientific research as well as promoting local and international co-operation, aiming to render services to the local community.

How It Works ?

Information Systems

our students study an array of scientific disciplines,including: data structures, file organization, numerical computing methods, system analysis and design,database management systems, economics, software development, management information systems,information engineering, electronic commerce..

Computer Science

our students study the developments of computers as well as designing and measuring the degree of complexity of the algorithms used to solve real problems. Moreover,the Department focuses on applications of artificial intelligence in which computers simulate the behavior of man in processing information and Other areas.

Basic Science

our students study an array of scientific disciplines,including: mathematics, physics, electronics, English language, computational linguistics, humanities, business management, report writing, Professional ethics & legal aspects..
